## üìä Documented Use Cases

### Mapping a Complex Hospital Information System

**Context**: GHT (Groupement Hospitalier de Territoire - Territorial Hospital Group) with multiple facilities

**Challenges**:
- Heterogeneous systems
- HDS (Health Data Hosting) requirements
- Limited budgets

**Solutions provided by Mercator**:
- Consolidated multi-site view
- ANSSI and HDS compliance

---

### Cloud Migration in Education

**Context**: University with 20,000 students

**Objectives**:
- Inventory before cloud migration
- Identification of dependencies
- Gradual migration plan

**Results**:
- Complete mapping in 3 months
- Identification of 15% obsolete applications
- Substantial savings on the migration

---

### Security Audit in a SME

**Context**: Industrial SME with 150 employees

**Needs**:
- ISO 27001 audit preparation
- Critical asset mapping
- IT system documentation

**Benefits**:
- Successful audit on the first attempt
- Increased visibility for management
- Foundation for ongoing management
